The International Research Collaboration for Marine Atmospheric Boundary Layer and Air–Sea Interactions (IRC-MABL-ASI) Workshop will be held on 22–23 June 2026 in the International Conference Room, Building 25-1, at Seoul National University (SNU).

The workshop is organized by Prof. Jung-Hoon Kim, Prof. SungHyun Nam, Prof. Hanna Na, and Prof. Sang-Moo Lee from the School of Earth and Environmental Sciences (SEES) at SNU.

The main objectives of this workshop are to identify shared scientific challenges, foster new opportunities for international collaboration, and establish a sustainable global research network led by early-career researchers.

*If you have not received an email about this workshop and would like to register, please send an email to the address provided before registering via Google Forms. We will check availability and respond to your inquiry accordingly.*
